Columns in users table:
- id (int)
- username (varchar(50))
- email (varchar(100))
- hashed_password (varchar(255))
- role (varchar(20))
- is_active (tinyint(1))
- is_approved (tinyint(1))
- full_name (varchar(100))
- status (enum('pending','approved','rejected'))
- created_at (timestamp)
- updated_at (timestamp)
